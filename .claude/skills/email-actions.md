# Skill: Email Operations (Nodemailer)

## send_counselor_toolkit
- **Recipient:** School counselors.
- **Content:** A PDF overview of the student's AI-generated pathway, apprenticeship links, and contact info for local partners.

## send_parent_guide
- **Recipient:** Parents/Guardians.
- **Content:** Financial aid basics, ROI comparison of the chosen career path, and "How to support your student" tips.

## session_summary
- **Triggers:** Automatically at the end of every successful `/api/session/end` call.
- **Logic:** Summarize the conversation, specific action items, and the next scheduled call.
